About me
Educator. Researcher. Lifelong Learner.

I am an Assistant Professor in Computer Science with over 13 years of teaching experience at the undergraduate and postgraduate levels. My academic home is the P.G. Department of Computer Science at Sree Sankara Vidyapeetom College, Valayanchirangara, Perumbavoor, Kerala.

My teaching spans Computer Fundamentals, Database Management Systems (DBMS), and Natural Language Processing (NLP) — subjects I teach not just as theory, but as living, evolving disciplines that shape the way we interact with technology.

I am currently pursuing my PhD at Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam, with research focused on Malayalam-based intermediate cipher design integrated with traditional encryption algorithms — work that sits at the intersection of natural language processing, Malayalam computing, and cryptographic security.

I serve as a Member of the Post Graduate Board of Studies in Computer Science at Mahatma Gandhi University, and am an active member of ACM and the Soft Computing Research Society (SCRS).
